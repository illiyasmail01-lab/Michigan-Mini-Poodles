Parent dogs

Ask for parent photos, temperament, and records.

Parent details can change from litter to litter. This page keeps the process professional by listing what families should confirm directly before reserving a puppy.

Before reserving

What to request.

01

Current parent photos

Ask for recent photos or videos of the dam and sire for the specific litter.

02

Adult size and coat notes

Confirm expected size range, color notes, grooming needs, and coat history.

03

Temperament

Ask how each parent behaves at home, around people, and during handling.

04

Records and registration

Request available testing, vet records, registration details, and contract terms.

Health records

Keep records easy to find.

Parent testing and vet care details can live on the health page now, with room for a Google Doc or Drive link later.

Health & Vet Care

Dam

Mother details

Request the dam's current photo, weight, temperament, registration information, and any available health or genetic testing details for the active litter.

Sire

Father details

Request the sire's current photo, size, temperament, registration information, and any available health or genetic testing details for the active litter.
